About the Game

Infinity Core is a deep nuclear reactor simulator where players must maintain a high-tech energy core in a volatile environment. The fate of the facility—and possibly the world—depends on your ability to stabilize core temperature, pressure, and radiation output while reacting to threats like power surges, earthquakes, cyberattacks, and operator errors.

Game Features

  • 🔥 Realistic meltdown simulation & emergency protocols
  • 🧠 AI-powered dynamic system failures
  • 🛠 Customizable reactor components
  • 📈 Advanced monitoring dashboards with live feedback
  • 🧯 Fire suppression, coolant leaks, and overvoltage systems

Core Systems You Must Manage

Temperature Regulation

Balance fuel rods, coolant levels, and ventilation fans to keep temperature stable under 1000°C.

Coolant System

Monitor pumps, flow sensors, and leak detection modules. Too little coolant leads to meltdown.

Radiation Shielding

Maintain shielding integrity. Radiation spikes can disable nearby systems and kill staff.

Power Output

Balance energy output to the grid while ensuring internal systems remain powered.
